# Milestone Report: Expand Ecosystem with New Projects (Q1 2026)

## Milestone Description
*   **Goal:** Expand Ecosystem with New Projects
*   **Contract Milestone:** Yes
*   **Acceptance Criteria:** Produce a “New Projects on Cardano” spotlight report featuring 2–3 projects (quarterly).
*   **Due Date:** Q1 2026

## Achievement Summary
The Developer Advocates fulfilled this milestone by conducting spotlight sessions and producing documentation for **TxPipe** and **Konma Lab (HaskLedger)**. These projects represent significant advancements in Cardano's developer infrastructure, edge computing, and community-driven talent retention.

## Featured Projects

### 1. TxPipe (TX3)
**TxPipe** is a major contributor to Cardano open-source tooling, focusing on high-performance infrastructure. Their latest project, **TX3**, introduces a high-level language for describing UTXO protocols.
*   **Focus:** Developer Experience, Protocol-as-API, DSLs, Tooling (Trix CLI).
*   **Session Highlights:** A deep dive into the TX3 language, visualization levels, and how it simplifies the transition from user intent to on-chain validation.

**[Read TxPipe (TX3) Spotlight Report](./txpipe_spotlight_q1_2026.md)**

### 2. Konma & HaskLedger
**Konma Lab** is bridging the gap between education and high-tech employment through their **HaskLedger** infrastructure and the "Transfer of Skills" initiative.
*   **Focus:** Infrastructure, Edge Computing (RISC-V), Education-to-Employment (Venture Building).
*   **Session Highlights:** Discussion on bringing edge computing to Cardano via RISC-V hardware and the architecture of the HaskLedger eDSL.
