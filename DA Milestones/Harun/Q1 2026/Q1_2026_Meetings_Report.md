### 2. Deep Dive – Konma Team (Vinit & Vinay)

**Attendees:** Vinit  
**Date:** Friday, January 23, 2026  
**Time:** 12:30 PM – 1:30 PM IST  
**Platform:** Google Meet

**Meeting Goal:**
To understand Konma's suite of blockchain and AI governance initiatives and explore collaboration opportunities within the Intersect ecosystem.

**Summary:**
Vinit presented **MyOS**, an internal operating system designed to verify tasks and data, with applications across the Haskell and Carver ledgers. Vinay introduced **Carbon Ledger**, a multi-chain framework focused on environmental measurements and verifiable compute using RISC-V. The team also demonstrated their AI governance architecture, which integrates AI-driven decision-making with human oversight across their trust stack.

The conversation then shifted to collaboration opportunities within Intersect. Harun outlined the **Developer Advocate Program** and the **Maintainer Retainer Program**, explaining how they incentivize open-source contributions while allowing contributors to retain their intellectual property. Harun encouraged Vinit to attend Intersect committee meetings, particularly the Open Source Committee, to build visibility and community trust.

Potential collaboration with MLabs was discussed, along with strategies for securing funding and demonstrating project utility to the broader Cardano community. The session wrapped up with a discussion on blockchain education challenges: both Emergo Academy's difficulties in attracting students due to cost and accessibility, and the wider talent-building challenge in the African market.

**Action Items:**

Vinit agreed to share the MyOS documentation and add Harun as a collaborator on the Plutarch starter project repository. Vinay committed to completing HaskLedger by mid-February as a milestone for future funding applications. Harun agreed to introduce the Konma team at Open Source Committee meetings.
