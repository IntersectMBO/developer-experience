## Evidence of Activities

### 1. Japanese Translation of the State of Developer Experience Survey

To make the 2025 State of Developer Experience Survey accessible to Japanese-speaking developers in the Cardano ecosystem, I undertook a structured translation process:

The survey was first translated into Japanese using **Gemini AI** as the primary translation tool. The output was then cross-referenced against a second pass using **Claude** to identify and resolve inconsistencies, improve phrasing, and ensure technical terminology was rendered accurately in Japanese. Following the AI-assisted drafts, I sought native speaker review, reaching out to **Fred Tanaka** and **Yuri** for feedback. In parallel, **Tex** facilitated an introduction to **Yuta**, who reviewed the translated survey in the Developer Experience Watercooler channel and provided approval on behalf of the Japanese-speaking community.

**Translated Survey:** [2025-State-of-Developer-Experience-JP.md](./2025-State-of-Developer-Experience-JP.md)
