### Onboarding Report: Developer Engagement & Integration – Q1 2026

#### Overview
During this quarter, I focused on onboarding developers into the Intersect ecosystem, specifically through the Developer Experience Working Group (Dev Ex WG) and direct contributions to core repositories. The goal was to transition community members from passive observers to active contributors and facilitate strategic professional connections within the Cardano ecosystem.

#### Developer Engagement Summary
- **2 developers** (Peter and Daniel) successfully onboarded as Dev Ex WG attendees and active contributors to core Intersect repositories.
- **12 additional developers** onboarded as core repository collaborators following the Cardano Hub Nairobi Hackathon.
- **1 designer** (Prestone) engaged and linked with a strategic opportunity within the Cardano Design Center (45B.io).
- Facilitated direct communication between new contributors and core project maintainers to streamline the review and merging process.
