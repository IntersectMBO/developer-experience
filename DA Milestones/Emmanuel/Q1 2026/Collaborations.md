# Collaboration Facilitation Report – Q1
**Developer Advocate – Intersect Open Source Office (OSO)**

## Overview

As part of the milestone to facilitate **2–3 introductory meetings between Cardano projects and potential collaborators**, I organized and supported discussions connecting ecosystem stakeholders and identifying opportunities for collaboration.

Two collaboration introductions were facilitated during this period:

1. Intersect Open Source Office × Blockchain Center Nairobi  
2. Cardano Design Center Initiative × Ecosystem Stakeholders  

These introductions were intended to strengthen collaboration across developer communities, ecosystem support initiatives, and organizations contributing to the Cardano ecosystem.


# Collaboration 1: Intersect OSO × Blockchain Center Nairobi

**Collaboration Type:** Community Development & Developer Enablement

## Introduction

An introductory meeting was facilitated between:

- **Darlington** – President, Blockchain Center Nairobi  
- **TEX** – Secretary, Intersect Open Source Office  

The objective of this meeting was to explore a **strategic partnership between Intersect MBO and Blockchain Center Nairobi**, focused on expanding developer education, strengthening open-source participation, and supporting ecosystem growth within the Cardano community.

Blockchain Center Nairobi is a regional hub dedicated to blockchain education, developer enablement, and community innovation. The organization regularly hosts training sessions, technical meetups, and developer programs, making it a strong partner for expanding Cardano's developer presence in East Africa.


## Strategic Partnership Rationale

### Benefits for Intersect

- Direct access to a growing pipeline of African developers
- On-the-ground presence for Cardano education and experimentation with open-source tooling
- A trusted hub for hosting governance discussions, workshops, and ecosystem activities
- Increased adoption of Cardano-based standards, tools, and frameworks

### Benefits for Blockchain Center Nairobi

- Access to global expertise and participation in Intersect working groups
- Opportunities for members to engage with governance and technical initiatives
- Increased resources for developer training, events, and open-source participation
- Stronger positioning as a Cardano-aligned innovation hub in East Africa


## Partnership Opportunity Areas

Several potential areas of collaboration were identified:

### Developer Education & Capacity Building

- Co-host Cardano developer workshops
- Establish local study groups for Haskell, Aiken, and Rust
- Support onboarding of developers into Cardano open-source projects
- Exchange trainers, contributors, and educational resources

### Community & Ecosystem Growth

- Establish a Cardano / Intersect community hub presence within Blockchain Center Nairobi
- Host community meetups and governance discussions
- Support onboarding of new developers into Cardano repositories

### Events & Hackathons

- Organize hackathons and developer competitions
- Run open-source contribution events
- Build a local contributor pipeline for Cardano repositories


## Outcome

One immediate outcome of this collaboration was the organization of a **Cardano Contribution Hackathon**, hosted in partnership with **Blockchain Center Nairobi**.

The event focused on:

- Introducing developers to Cardano open-source repositories
- Encouraging contributions to Intersect and ecosystem projects
- Providing hands-on experience with GitHub contribution workflows

The hackathon helped establish a **local developer contribution pipeline** and strengthened collaboration between the Nairobi blockchain community and the broader Cardano ecosystem.

# Collaboration 2: Cardano Design Center Initiative × Ecosystem Stakeholders

**Collaboration Type:** Ecosystem Support & Product Design

## Introduction

A second collaboration introduction was facilitated involving the **Cardano Design Center initiative**, a funded proposal titled:

**“45B: Cardano Design Center to Supporting Projects.”**

Proposal Reference:  
https://www.catalystexplorer.com/en/proposals/45b-cardano-design-center-to-supporting-projects-f13/details

The initiative aims to provide **design, product strategy, and usability support** to Cardano ecosystem projects in order to improve application quality, developer experience, and user adoption.

As part of this facilitation effort, I introduced a **designer associated with the Cardano Design Center initiative** to ecosystem stakeholders interested in improving developer and user experience across Cardano-based applications and tools.


## Discussion Areas

The discussion focused on how the **Cardano Design Center initiative** could support ecosystem builders through:

- **UX/UI design consultation**
- **Product and marketing strategy support**
- **User journey and usability improvements**
- **Developer and community onboarding design**
- **Localization and accessibility strategies**
- **Community education around Cardano concepts such as UTxO, staking, and smart contracts**

These services aim to address a common challenge in early-stage blockchain projects, where strong technical development is often not matched by strong usability or product design.


## Outcome

This introduction helped raise awareness of the **Cardano Design Center initiative** and its potential to support Cardano projects through design and product guidance.

By connecting **design-focused contributors with technical builders**, this collaboration contributes to improving:

- usability of Cardano decentralized applications
- onboarding experiences for new users
- overall quality and accessibility of ecosystem products
